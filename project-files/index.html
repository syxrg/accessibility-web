IT Accessibility Solutions for Everyone!

Welcome! "A11y" is a shorthand notation for "accessibility." The number "11" represents the eleven letters between "A" and "Y" in the word "accessibility." If you find web accessibility requirements daunting, you're not alone. However, with the right knowledge and guidance, implementing accessibility doesn't have to be a mystery or a costly affair.

Understanding the principles involved can empower you and your team to create rich, interactive, and accessible web experiences.
